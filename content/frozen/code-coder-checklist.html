Discovery Checklist · CODE–CODER

Questions the PM
and Architect must have answered

Before a CODE-CODER engagement can be called ready, two sets of answers must be in hand: from the customer about their business and existing setup, and from the OEM provider about the platform's capabilities and commitments. Walk every node; tick every box; copy a section into your discovery doc when done.

Part One

CODE — Business Layer

The four domains that hold every digital engagement. Lose track of any one and the delivery wobbles.

C
Content
Content model · ownership · governance · locale
Ask the customer
  • What content types do you have today, and how are they organised into a hierarchy?
  • Where does each type originate — authored, syndicated, fed in, user-generated?
  • How often does each type change or need refreshing?
  • Who creates, approves and publishes content — separate owners or the same people?
  • Which locales do you support, and how is translation sourced and kept in sync?
  • What metadata fields does each content type carry today?
  • Where do assets live now — DAM, file shares, S3 buckets, scattered?
  • What rights, licences and expiry windows apply to which assets?
  • What is your current SEO strategy and tooling?
  • What content performs, what converts, and what should be retired?
  • What governance workflows exist for sensitive content (legal, compliance)?
  • What is the volume of content (count, GB), and growth rate?
Ask the OEM provider
  • Which content types are out of the box, and which require custom modelling?
  • What is the authoring experience for non-technical users?
  • What localisation capabilities are native — fallbacks, machine translation, workflow?
  • What is the API surface for headless content delivery?
  • What governance, workflow and approval features ship by default?
  • How is versioning, rollback and scheduling handled?
  • Storage limits and pricing model — per asset, per GB, per request?
  • Migration tools from our current CMS — and reference customers who have done it?
  • What is the roadmap for AI-assisted authoring and translation?
  • How is content cached, invalidated and delivered at the edge?
  • SLAs on publishing latency and API availability?
  • Customer references at our scale and complexity?
O
Operations & Martech
Workflows · campaigns · analytics · tooling
Ask the customer
  • What is your current martech stack — AEM, Salesforce, HubSpot, anything else?
  • Which campaigns are highest in frequency, and which in revenue?
  • How do approvals flow today — tool, email, meeting?
  • What analytics tools are in use, and who uses them?
  • Who consumes reports, and at what cadence?
  • What is automated today, and what is still manual?
  • Where are your operational SLAs being missed?
  • What is the org chart for marketing operations?
  • How is consent and preference data captured and stored?
  • What audience segments matter most, and how are they defined?
  • Do you have a customer data platform — and is it the source of truth?
  • What manual steps would you most like to eliminate?
Ask the OEM provider
  • What native integrations exist with our current stack?
  • What does the workflow engine support — branching, parallel, approval gates?
  • What is the segmentation model and its scale limits?
  • Analytics export — push, pull, batch, real-time?
  • Cost model — per event, per profile, per query, per seat?
  • Compliance certifications — SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA?
  • Audit trail of operational changes — who did what, when?
  • Multi-region operations and data residency controls?
  • Sandbox environments for operations testing?
  • API rate limits relevant to operational workflows?
  • Roadmap for agentic / AI-assisted operations?
  • Disaster recovery and failover guarantees?
D
Design & Data
UX · journeys · data model · measurement
Ask the customer
  • Who are your users — what personas exist today?
  • What are the top three user journeys by volume? By value?
  • Where are users dropping off or struggling?
  • What design system or brand assets are already in place?
  • Accessibility standard required — WCAG 2.1 AA, AAA, EN 301 549?
  • What data do you currently collect from users, and why?
  • Where is consent captured, and how granular is it?
  • How is data shared across systems — point-to-point, hub, CDP?
  • What personalisation is in place today, and how is it measured?
  • What KPIs define success for this engagement?
  • Who owns research and discovery findings inside the business?
  • Are there regulatory constraints on data use (financial, health, children)?
Ask the OEM provider
  • What is the canonical data model — entities, relationships, identifiers?
  • How is PII handled — encryption at rest, in transit, masking?
  • Personalisation capabilities — rules, segments, ML models, real-time?
  • Data residency options and cross-border transfer mechanisms?
  • Export and migration paths — full dump, incremental, format?
  • Identity resolution capabilities — known, anonymous, deterministic, probabilistic?
  • Are the admin UIs themselves accessible?
  • Audit logs for data access and changes?
  • Retention controls and right-to-be-forgotten support?
  • Test-data generation and anonymisation tooling?
  • SDKs for design system integration?
  • What's the platform's stance on first-party data in a cookieless world?
E
Engineering
Architecture · teams · constraints · stack
Ask the customer
  • What is your current architecture — diagrams, sequence flows, ownership?
  • Which teams own which systems, and what is the change cadence?
  • What is the code review and deploy process today?
  • What technical debt exists, and which pieces are blocking?
  • Non-negotiable constraints — cloud, language, framework, vendor?
  • How is your engineering team sized — internal, partner, mixed?
  • What AI-assisted development tooling is already in use?
  • What documentation exists, and is it current?
  • How is knowledge transferred when people leave?
  • What security review process must our work pass?
  • What testing standards are required before merge?
  • What is your appetite for migration vs strangler-pattern adoption?
Ask the OEM provider
  • Supported deployment models — SaaS, PaaS, on-prem, hybrid?
  • SDKs and language support — official, community, deprecated?
  • Extension model — APIs, plugins, custom code, sandboxed scripts?
  • Performance benchmarks at our anticipated scale?
  • Reference architecture documentation for our use case?
  • How are platform upgrades handled — opt-in, forced, blue/green?
  • What is the developer experience — local dev, debugging, observability?
  • Source-control integration and CI/CD compatibility?
  • Type definitions, schemas and contracts available as artefacts?
  • Customer-facing engineering support model and response times?
  • Open standards used vs proprietary extensions?
  • Total cost of ownership including development and operations?

P
Platform Architecture
AEM EDS · AEMaaCS · Commerce · Stack selection
Ask the customer
  • Are you evaluating Edge Delivery Services, AEM as a Cloud Service, or both — and what is driving that evaluation today?
  • Who authors content — marketing power users, developers, or a distributed editorial team — and what is their technical comfort level?
  • Is document-based authoring in SharePoint or Google Drive acceptable, or do authors require a traditional AEM page editor?
  • What is your content publishing velocity — how many pages or components change per day, and does a static-first delivery model hold at that cadence?
  • What CDN do you currently run, and does consolidating to EDS's managed CDN fit your security and procurement constraints?
  • What are your personalisation requirements — segment-level, 1:1, or rule-based — and do they require server-side rendering at edge or can they be deferred to client-side?
  • Are you on AEM 6.x on-premises, AEM Managed Services, or already on AEMaaCS — and what is the migration trigger: end-of-support, cost pressure, or feature gap?
  • Who holds the AEM licence — your organisation or the agency — and what are the renewal and cost-allocation constraints?
  • What is your tolerance for fortnightly platform auto-updates: do you have a regression test suite that can gate a fortnightly Cloud Manager pipeline?
  • Is this commerce deployment B2B, B2C, or a mixed model — and if B2B, how many buyer organisations, cost centres, and contract pricing tiers are in scope?
  • What is your current and projected order volume — average daily orders and peak orders-per-minute during promotions?
  • Which commerce platform is in scope — Adobe Commerce, Salesforce Commerce Cloud, Commercetools, or another — and is the storefront headless today or tightly coupled to the platform?
  • Who owns the checkout experience — a PSP-hosted page, a custom implementation, or a third-party accelerator?
  • What is the combined budget for platform licensing across AEM and Commerce, and is that budget fixed or tied to a business case that needs validating?
Ask the OEM provider
  • What are the contractual performance SLAs for EDS — specifically TTFB and LCP targets, and what remedies apply if those are missed?
  • What personalisation options are native to EDS, and at what point does the architecture require a third-party decisioning engine or client-side injection?
  • What is the EDS CDN topology — which PoP network does it run on — and can customers bring an existing Akamai, Fastly, or Cloudflare contract in front of it?
  • How quickly does a content change in SharePoint or Google Drive propagate to the edge, and what is the revalidation mechanism — pull, push, or webhook-triggered purge?
  • Which authoring surfaces are officially supported by EDS today — SharePoint, Google Drive, Universal Editor — and what is the roadmap for any that are still in beta?
  • What is the AEMaaCS auto-update policy — can a release window be deferred, and if so for how long and under what support agreement tier?
  • What is the published uptime SLA for AEMaaCS publish tier, author tier, and the Assets API separately, and are those covered by the same or different SLA credits?
  • How is Dispatcher configuration managed in AEMaaCS — is it code-deployed via Cloud Manager, and what rules cannot be overridden by a customer?
  • What is the Cloud Manager environment limit per programme, what does an additional non-production environment cost, and is there a sandbox tier for proof-of-concept work?
  • What is the supported migration path and tooling from AEM 6.5 to AEMaaCS — what does the BPA report cover, and what is a realistic migration timeline for a 50 000-page site?
  • What is the uptime SLA on commerce checkout and storefront APIs, and what is the compensation model when that SLA is breached during a peak promotional window?
  • What are the rate limits on storefront and cart APIs at peak load, and what is the process for requesting a temporary burst allowance ahead of a planned promotion?
  • How is a B2B account hierarchy — buyer organisation, sub-accounts, cost centres, and contract-specific pricing — modelled in the platform's native data model, and what requires customisation?
  • What payment gateways are natively certified on the platform, and what is the integration path for a PSP not on that list — certified extension, custom module, or not supported?
Part Two

CODER — Inside Engineering

The five layers an engineering lead must hold honest. Each one needs answers from both the client and any platform vendor whose work sits inside it.

C
Code
FE · BE · integration · structure · standards
Ask the customer
  • What coding standards and style guides are in use?
  • What frameworks and libraries are already adopted, and which are off-limits?
  • What is the expected test coverage threshold?
  • Team experience level — senior, mixed, junior-heavy?
  • What AI / agentic coding tools are sanctioned for use?
  • What is the branching and merge strategy?
  • Is there an architecture review board, and what does it review?
  • What logging, monitoring and observability stack is in place?
  • How is technical documentation maintained?
  • What is the codebase's current state — greenfield, brownfield, legacy lift?
  • Are there feature-flag or experimentation systems in place?
  • Who owns the front-end design system and component library?
Ask the OEM provider
  • Is the component library open source, proprietary, or both?
  • Quality and currency of SDK documentation?
  • Reference implementations available for our archetypes?
  • Versioning policy — semver, deprecation timelines, LTS?
  • Support for agentic codegen workflows (Stalwart-style)?
  • Customer-facing developer portal and community?
  • Code samples that are tested and maintained alongside the product?
  • Is the platform's own code inspectable, or pure black box?
  • Backward compatibility commitments across major versions?
  • Mocking and local-dev support for offline work?
  • Telemetry and crash-reporting baked into SDKs?
  • Custom-code extension points and their performance implications?
O
Optimization & Quality
Performance · testing · security · accessibility
Ask the customer
  • What performance budgets must be met — TTI, LCP, INP, CLS, weight?
  • What test environments are available, and how are they kept fresh?
  • What security review process gates production?
  • Required accessibility level, and how is it tested?
  • What load patterns must the system handle — peak, sustained, spike?
  • What is the current error budget or SLO?
  • How is quality measured today, and by whom?
  • What tooling exists for performance profiling and tracing?
  • What is the policy on third-party scripts and their performance cost?
  • Penetration testing cadence and last-known findings?
  • Are accessibility audits done internally or by a partner?
  • Where have past performance incidents come from?
Ask the OEM provider
  • Platform performance SLAs — uptime, latency, throughput?
  • Built-in caching, CDN and edge capabilities?
  • Security certifications and last pen-test results?
  • Accessibility compliance of admin UIs and outputs?
  • Status page history — incidents, frequency, transparency?
  • Vulnerability disclosure programme and patch cadence?
  • Bug-bounty programme — exists, scope, payout?
  • Data encryption — at rest, in transit, key management?
  • Performance tooling available to customers — RUM, synthetic, traces?
  • Auto-scaling behaviour and cost implications?
  • Test environments — quality, parity with prod, refresh cadence?
  • What happens during a platform incident — comms, mitigation, RCA?
D
Deployment
Pipelines · environments · automation · rollback
Ask the customer
  • Where will the system run — cloud, region, on-prem, hybrid?
  • What CI/CD pipeline is in place, and what gates exist?
  • Environment strategy — dev, stage, UAT, prod, ephemeral previews?
  • Rollback expectations — time, mechanism, who has the button?
  • Database migration strategy — backward compatible by default?
  • Secrets management — vault, env vars, KMS?
  • Infrastructure-as-code adoption and tooling?
  • Are deployments self-service or gated by ops?
  • Change-management process — ITIL, CAB, lightweight?
  • Mean time to recovery target?
  • How are credentials rotated and what is the policy?
  • What deployment failures have hurt in the last 12 months?
Ask the OEM provider
  • Hosting model — SaaS, PaaS, customer-managed, BYO-cloud?
  • Deployment automation tooling, and how customer code reaches it?
  • Environment provisioning lead time and self-service controls?
  • Blue/green, canary or progressive rollout support?
  • How are platform upgrades scheduled, and who can defer them?
  • Backup, snapshot and restore guarantees?
  • Multi-region deployment options and active-active capabilities?
  • Promotion path between environments — config diff, content diff, code diff?
  • Audit log of deployments and configuration changes?
  • Cost model around environments — included, charged, capped?
  • Network controls — VPC peering, private link, allowlists?
  • Compliance with the customer's required cloud provider and region?
E
External Integrations
APIs · auth · partners · failure modes
Ask the customer
  • What systems must this integrate with — CRM, ERP, payment, identity, search, AI?
  • Authentication strategy — SSO, OAuth, SAML, OIDC, custom?
  • Is there an existing API gateway, and is it mandatory?
  • Data exchange formats and event protocols already in use?
  • Who owns each external system — internal team, third party, both?
  • What are the SLAs of those systems, and where do they break?
  • Are sandbox environments available for every integration?
  • What happens when an external system is down — graceful degrade or hard fail?
  • Is there a service registry or catalogue of integrations?
  • What integration patterns are sanctioned — sync, async, event, batch?
  • Volume expectations per integration — requests per day, payload size?
  • Compliance constraints on cross-system data movement?
Ask the OEM provider
  • Native connectors and their coverage of standard third parties?
  • Authentication options on the platform side, and their constraints?
  • Rate limits on integration APIs, and how they're communicated?
  • Webhook reliability, retry policy and replay support?
  • Support for legacy protocols where needed — SOAP, EDI, FTP?
  • Asynchronous integration patterns — pub/sub, queues, events?
  • How are integration failures surfaced — logs, alerts, dashboards?
  • SLA on platform's integration availability separate from product SLA?
  • Idempotency and exactly-once guarantees where they matter?
  • Bulk and incremental sync capabilities?
  • Versioning of integration APIs and deprecation policy?
  • Partner ecosystem and certified integrators?
N
Networking & API Gateway
CDN · gateway · latency · topology · DNS
Ask the customer
  • Do you have an existing CDN contract, and which vendor — Akamai, Cloudflare, Fastly, or AWS CloudFront?
  • Is the CDN choice mandated by procurement or security policy, or can it be changed for this project?
  • What is your current CDN cache hit rate, and is it actively measured and reported on?
  • Are you on shared CDN infrastructure or a dedicated tenant, and does your contract allow you to change that?
  • What is the CDN cost model — flat fee, per-GB egress, or request-based — and what are the overage charges?
  • Is there a corporate API gateway mandate, and which product — Kong, AWS API GW, Azure APIM, or Apigee?
  • What authentication is enforced at the gateway today — mTLS, API key, OAuth, or a combination?
  • Do you operate a service registry or API catalogue, and who is responsible for keeping it current?
  • Which team owns the API gateway — a central platform team, an infra team, or individual squads?
  • What rate limiting policies are in place today, and are they enforced at the gateway or per-service?
  • Do you have private network requirements — VPC peering, private endpoints, ExpressRoute, or Direct Connect?
  • Are there firewall rules or egress restrictions that affect outbound calls from application servers?
  • Do you have IP allowlisting requirements for external integrations, and who manages that list?
  • Who controls DNS, what is the TTL policy, and are there approval workflows that add latency to propagation?
  • Do you operate in regions with data residency requirements that constrain how traffic is routed or where it terminates?
  • What are your latency budgets per API call tier — internal service-to-service, external customer-facing, and third-party integrations?
Ask the OEM provider
  • What is the cache invalidation mechanism — URL-based, tag-based, or API-triggered — and which tiers of service include it?
  • What is the guaranteed propagation time from an invalidation request to all edge nodes confirming the cache is cleared?
  • Is the CDN WAF included at the contracted tier, or is it a separately licensed add-on with its own pricing?
  • Can customers bring their own TLS certificates and configure custom domains, and is there a limit on the number of domains?
  • Do you publish a stable CDN IP range list, and how are customers notified if that list changes?
  • What API gateway is included or recommended at the contracted price tier, and what does it cost to upgrade?
  • What authentication schemes does the platform's API natively enforce, and which require custom middleware?
  • What are the burst rate limits per endpoint, how are they surfaced to clients — response header, portal alert, or proactive notification?
  • Is a developer portal and API catalogue included in the platform, or must the customer provision and maintain their own?
  • How are breaking API changes communicated, what notice period is guaranteed, and what is the deprecation lifecycle policy?
  • Does the platform support VPC peering or private connectivity — AWS PrivateLink, Azure Private Endpoint — to customer environments?
  • What are the platform's egress IP ranges that customers must allowlist, and how are changes to those ranges announced?
  • Does the platform manage DNS for the customer's domain, or does the customer retain full DNS control with a CNAME delegation only?
  • What is the DDoS protection model — volumetric scrubbing, rate limiting, or a managed service — and who is the escalation contact during an active attack?
A
Authentication & Authorisation
SSO · SAML · OAuth · OIDC · JWT · permissions
Ask the customer
  • How many distinct identity systems are in production today — Azure AD, Okta, Ping, custom LDAP, Cognito — and which is the system of record?
  • Is there a corporate IdP mandate, and which team owns the contract and configuration?
  • How many distinct user populations exist — internal employees, external customers, partners, B2B buyers — and do they authenticate against the same IdP?
  • Is enterprise SSO required for CMS author logins, customer-facing logins, or both?
  • What protocol is in use for current internal-tool SSO — SAML 2.0 or OIDC?
  • Are there federated identity requirements — multiple upstream IdPs, cross-domain identity brokering, or guest-identity flows?
  • Is the customer-facing site fully behind a login wall, fully public, or a mixed model with optional authentication?
  • What is the expected peak concurrent authenticated session count, and is that number contractually defined anywhere?
  • Is social login in scope — Google, Apple, LinkedIn — and if so, for which user populations?
  • What is the required account self-service scope — registration, email verification, password reset, MFA enrolment, account deletion?
  • Are there machine-to-machine integrations that require OAuth 2.0 client credentials flow, and which services are the clients?
  • Are JWTs in use today, and if so what is the current signing key rotation policy and who triggers rotation?
  • What OAuth scopes and OIDC claims must be carried in tokens to satisfy downstream service authorisation?
  • Is the permission model RBAC, ABAC, or flat ACL, and how many roles are anticipated at go-live?
  • Are permissions synchronised from IdP group membership, or managed independently inside the application layer?
  • Are there session timeout durations or MFA requirements mandated by the security policy, and do they differ by user population?

Reference the AEM × Banking FAQ for networking and compliance-specific auth patterns in regulated industries.

Ask the OEM provider
  • Which identity protocols does the platform support natively — SAML 2.0, OIDC, OAuth 2.0, LDAP — and which require a paid add-on or custom connector?
  • Are there certified, pre-built connectors for Azure AD, Okta, and Ping Identity, and what is the support tier for each?
  • What MFA methods are supported natively — TOTP, FIDO2/WebAuthn, SMS OTP, push notification — and which require third-party integration?
  • Does the platform support both SP-initiated and IdP-initiated SAML flows, and are there known limitations with either?
  • Can the platform act as both a SAML SP and a SAML IdP in the same deployment?
  • What SAML attribute mappings are configurable in the assertion, and is that configuration self-service or requires a support ticket?
  • Does the platform support OAuth 2.0 authorisation code flow with PKCE for single-page application clients?
  • What are the default access token and refresh token lifetimes, and are they configurable per client or per application?
  • Is refresh token rotation supported, and what is the behaviour on concurrent use of a rotated token?
  • Does the platform expose a JWKS endpoint for public key retrieval, and what is the key rollover process?
  • Are platform-issued JWTs signed with RS256 or HS256, is the algorithm configurable, and what custom claims can be injected?
  • What is the session management model — server-side session store, stateless JWT, or cookie — and is that configurable per deployment?
  • Is there built-in credential stuffing defence, bot protection, and account-takeover detection — rate limiting, anomaly scoring, IP blocking — or does that require an external WAF?
  • Is SAML single logout implemented, and does it propagate logout to all federated sessions?
  • Is SCIM 2.0 provisioning supported for automated user lifecycle management from the upstream IdP?
  • What are the documented rate limits on authentication endpoints — logins per second, token issuance per minute — and what is the SLA for auth availability independently of the application tier?
R
Release Management
Readiness · cadence · hypercare · rollback
Ask the customer
  • Is there a change advisory board or release approval process?
  • Release windows and blackout periods (peak season, freeze)?
  • How are customer-facing changes communicated, and by whom?
  • Rollback SLA — minutes, hours, what counts as "rolled back"?
  • Hypercare expectations after go-live — duration, staffing, response?
  • What does "production-ready" mean in your organisation, formally?
  • Who signs off on go-live, and what evidence do they need?
  • Training requirements for end users and operators?
  • How is success measured in the first 30, 60, 90 days?
  • What is the escalation path during a release weekend?
  • How are external stakeholders (partners, regulators) involved?
  • Has a comparable release ever gone badly — what was learned?
Ask the OEM provider
  • Platform release cadence — weekly, monthly, opt-in?
  • Breaking change policy and notification lead time?
  • Deprecation timelines and customer-impact assessments?
  • Hypercare and support model post-launch?
  • Customer Success engagement during major releases?
  • Release notes quality — granularity, channels, machine-readable?
  • Beta and early-access programmes — who qualifies, what is committed?
  • Rollback capabilities on the platform side?
  • What is the platform's own release-readiness checklist?
  • How are platform incidents during a customer release handled?
  • Reference launches at our scale — case studies, customer contacts?
  • Long-term roadmap visibility under NDA?
