Verify a Certificate

Enter a CCA-F certificate ID to confirm it is genuine. All certificates are cryptographically signed at issue time. Anyone can verify — no login required.

Certificate IDs follow the format CCA-F-YYYYMMDD-XXXXXXXX and appear on the certificate PDF and on the candidate's history page. Test codes (AOC-...) are separate and not verifiable here.
